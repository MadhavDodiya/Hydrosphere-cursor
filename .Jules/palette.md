## 2024-04-26 - Add missing aria-label to icon-only toggle buttons\n**Learning:** Icon-only buttons used for layout toggling (e.g., sidebar) require explicit `aria-label` attributes to be accessible to screen readers, and decorative inner icons should use `aria-hidden="true"` to prevent redundant readout.\n**Action:** Always verify icon-only buttons have descriptive ARIA labels and ensure purely decorative font icons within them are hidden from assistive technologies.
